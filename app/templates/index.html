Climate Cues

Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time, and the impact is being felt across the globe—especially in countries like India. But how much do we really understand about our role in it? Cool the Globe is here to help you discover just that.

Our Goal

Our goal is to spread awareness about global warming, carbon footprints, and how our everyday choices contribute to these issues. Through engaging quizzes, educational resources, and practical tips, we empower you to make informed decisions for a more sustainable future.

We believe that every action counts. Whether it's reducing energy consumption, switching to renewable energy, or adopting eco-friendly practices, each step brings us closer to cooling the planet. Join us on this journey of awareness and change. Together, we can Cool the Globe—one mindful action at a time.

Carbon Footprint

Do you know how much carbon an individual emits in a given span of time? That’s exactly what carbon footprint tells us!

A carbon footprint refers to the amount of greenhouse gases, particularly carbon dioxide (CO₂), that we release into the atmosphere through our daily activities. These gases trap heat and contribute to global warming, which is causing drastic changes to our planet’s climate. But the good news is, every one of us can make changes to reduce our carbon emissions and protect the environment.

Explore the resources below to learn how your everyday choices—like how you travel, what you eat, and the energy you consume—impact the planet, and discover practical ways to reduce your footprint.
